Privacy Policy

Welcome to Void Network (found at https://void.radio.am). We place a high value on user privacy. This policy provides details on how your information is collected, used, and managed, as well as your rights related to it.

1. Children's Privacy

Ensuring the privacy of children is of utmost importance to us. We do not intentionally gather personal data from users under the age of 13. If you become aware that your child has shared personal details, please notify us so we can promptly remove it.

2. Information Collection

Non-Personal Data: Our site automatically gathers certain data that doesn't identify you personally, such as IP addresses, browser types, pages you've visited, and how long you spend on them.

Personal Data: We only collect personal details—like names, emails, or phone numbers—if you willingly provide them.

3. Cookies and Tracking Tools

Explanation: Cookies are tiny data files that websites place on your device to remember preferences and enhance your browsing session.

Our Use of Cookies: We employ cookies to customize advertisements, enable social media functions, and analyze traffic patterns. You have the option to turn off cookies in your browser settings, although doing so may limit your site experience.

4. Usage of Collected Data

Advertising Purposes: We collaborate with external vendors, such as Google, to display advertisements. These vendors use cookies to show ads based on your activity across various sites, including ours.

Site Improvement: The data we gather helps us enhance and adjust our site's content and design for a better user experience.

5. Google AdSense and Ad Practices

Third-Party Services: Google, a third-party service provider, uses DART cookies to show relevant ads tailored to your browsing habits across multiple websites.

Opt-Out Option: You can choose not to participate in DART cookie tracking by visiting Google's Ad and Content Network privacy page.

6. External Links and Third-Party Policies

Our Privacy Policy does not cover other websites or advertisers that may be linked from our site. We recommend that you read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

7. How to Reach Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please get in touch with us through our contact page.
